## 1. Overview

The cabinet is the outdoor-rated enclosure that houses all charger subsystems. It provides physical protection (IP54+, IK10), thermal zoning, cable management, and a user-facing front panel with the HMI and connector holster.

## 4. Notes

- Cabinet weight (empty): ~150 kg; total system weight (150 kW config): ~460 kg
- Cabinet dimensions accommodate 4 thermal zones: control electronics, power modules, cooling, AC protection
- Front panel includes cutouts for HMI display, RFID reader, E-Stop button, connector holster, and status LEDs
- Cable entry from bottom or rear depending on installation requirements
- Outdoor rated for -30°C to +50°C ambient (with HVAC clip-on unit)
