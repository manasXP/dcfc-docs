## 1. Overview

The CCS2 connector and liquid-cooled cable assembly provide the physical and electrical interface between the charger and the electric vehicle. The assembly handles 500A at up to 1000V DC, with integrated cooling to manage thermal loads at high currents.

## 4. Notes

- The connector head and cable assembly are typically purchased as a pre-assembled, factory-tested unit including the temperature sensors, lock actuator, LED ring, and PP resistor
- Liquid cooling is required for sustained 500A operation — the coolant loop runs through internal channels in the cable conductors and connector pins
- The cable is a field-replaceable unit (FRU) with ~1 hour replacement time
- For a dual-connector configuration, double the quantities and cost
